## Part 1: Google Search Console Setup

### Step 1: Add Your Website to Google Search Console

1. **Go to Google Search Console:**
   - Visit: https://search.google.com/search-console/
   - Sign in with your Google account

2. **Add Property:**
   - Click "Add Property"
   - Select "URL prefix"
   - Enter: `https://anrfpairiitk.in`
   - Click "Continue"

3. **Verify Ownership:**

**Method 1: HTML File Upload (Easiest)**
- Google will give you an HTML file to download
- Upload it to your GitHub repository root
- Commit and push to GitHub
- Click "Verify" in Google Search Console

**Method 2: HTML Tag**
- Google will give you a meta tag like:
  ```html
  <meta name="google-site-verification" content="YOUR_CODE_HERE" />
  ```
- Add this to the `<head>` section of index.html
- Commit and push
- Click "Verify"

---

### Step 2: Submit Sitemap

1. **In Google Search Console:**
   - Go to "Sitemaps" (left sidebar)
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"

2. **Google will start crawling your site**
   - Usually takes 24-48 hours
   - Check "Coverage" to see indexed pages
